Mission Statement

Our goal is to offer exceptional tutoring that aids in student learning and equips students with the tools to attain success.

About us

Founded in 2013, Tutors R'US has a decade of experience assisting students of all abilities in reaching their academic objectives, from entrance exams to university acceptance. Our personal and tailored approach is led by experienced consultants who manage your educational needs from the initial assignment brief and tutor placement until desired results are achieved. We offer tutoring in all subjects, exams, and levels, including primary, entrance exams (7/8+, 11+, 13+, and 16+), GCSE, A-level, IB, Pre-U, university, and beyond. Tutors are available for in-home sessions at a time convenient for you and your child, including after school and weekends. We also offer residential and full-time tutors as needed, and provide online tutoring for international and university students.
